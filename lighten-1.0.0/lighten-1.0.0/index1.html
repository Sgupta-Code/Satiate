The Zero Hunger Generation




This video addresses the sustainability goal of “Zero Hunger.” It explores the reasons behind global hunger and proposes evidence-based solutions to achieve this goal.
It discusses global hunger and malnutrition despite food abundance. It highlights four factors affecting food security: availability, accessibility, stability, and utilization. The video suggests making food more affordable, accessible, and nutritious, and calls on the "zero hunger generation" to end global hunger.
It is presented in an informative and engaging way with animations and graphics.

"The Hidden Impact of Food Waste on Climate Change and Hunger"




This video discusses the global issue of food loss and waste, which accounts for 614 kcal per person every day, wasting resources and contributing to 8-10% of greenhouse gas emissions.

The video highlights the differences in causes and solutions between high-income and lower-income countries, emphasizing the need for improved soil quality, storage facilities, and consumer behavior changes.
Suggestions for reducing food waste include utilizing technology, embracing "ugly" food, and raising awareness about proper food storage and disposal.
